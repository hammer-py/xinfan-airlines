About Us

新帆航空 Xinfan AirlinesXinfan Airlines

新帆航空是一家成立于 Roblox 平台的虚拟航空公司，总部设于香港国际机场。我们模拟真实航空公司的运营模式，提供定期航班、机组人员招募、里程积累等服务。Xinfan Airlines is a virtual airline on the Roblox platform, headquartered at Hong Kong International Airport. We simulate real airline operations, offering scheduled flights, crew recruitment, and mileage programs.

无论是想体验飞行员驾驶乐趣的玩家，还是想要担任空乘、地勤的航空爱好者，都可以在新帆航空找到适合自己的角色。Whether you dream of being a pilot or want to serve as cabin crew, there's a role for every aviation enthusiast at Xinfan.

定期航班Scheduled Flights

覆盖全球主要枢纽的定期虚拟航班，拥有 22 架飞机的现代化机队，包括 B787-10、A350-900、B737-800 等机型。Scheduled virtual flights covering major global hubs with a modern fleet of 22 aircraft.

专业团队Professional Team

招募机长、空乘、地勤等虚拟航空人才。从实习到正式员工，提供完整的职业晋升通道和培训体系。Recruiting pilots, cabin crew, and ground staff with a complete career path from trainee to professional.

里程计划Mileage Program

每次飞行积累里程，兑换优先登机、贵宾休息室、自定义头衔、免费航班券等专属权益。Earn miles on every flight. Redeem for priority boarding, lounge access, custom titles, and free flight vouchers.
