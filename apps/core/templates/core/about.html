公司简介Company Profile

新帆航空（Xinfan Airlines）是一家成立于 Roblox 平台的虚拟航空公司，致力于为 Roblox 玩家提供真实、专业的虚拟航空体验。我们模拟真实航空公司的运营模式，提供定期航班、机组人员招募、里程积累等服务。Xinfan Airlines is a virtual airline on the Roblox platform, dedicated to providing authentic and professional virtual aviation experiences. We simulate real airline operations with scheduled flights, crew recruitment, and mileage programs.

无论是想体验飞行员驾驶乐趣的玩家，还是想要担任空乘、地勤的航空爱好者，都可以在新帆航空找到适合自己的角色。Whether you want to experience life as a pilot or serve as cabin crew or ground staff, Xinfan has a role for every aviation enthusiast.
